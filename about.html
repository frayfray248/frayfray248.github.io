About Me

I am a recent computer science diploma graduate from Camosun College. I am a dedicated and loyal worker with a strong enthusiasm for the problem-solving aspect of programming and seek to grow my skills as a developer.

I had the pleasure of working as developer and technical writer in a Camosun Capstone team that delivered a course manager WordPress plugin for Victoria companies Three Trees Technical and Oculus Design. As technical writer, I wrote clear and effective technical documentation such as the project charter, team biographies, user manual, and final report. As developer, I solely built the application’s API and heavily developed the WordPress front end.

I had an amazing experience in Camosun College’s Information and Computer Systems (ICS) Diploma program. There I gained the knowledge for analysis and development of practical IT solutions. I finished ICS with a GPA of 8.5.

I have a great interest in web applications and object-oriented programming. I aim to create fast and reliable services; organized and well-commented code; loosely coupled and scalable components; and simple and easy-to-use UI. Need to see some examples? Have a look at my projects or see my GitHub repositories.
