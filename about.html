About Me

I am a recent computer science diploma graduate. I am a dedicated and loyal worker with an enthusiasm for algorithms and seek to build my skills as a developer. I was raised in Victoria where I discovered my interest in computers. Being the computer wiz in my family, I was the one to solve technical problems for my parents and built my own custom content for games.

Eventually my enthusiasm for technology grew, and I enrolled in Camosun’s Information and Computer Systems (ICS) program. There I learned skills for analysis and development of practical IT solutions. I finished ICS with a GPA of 8.5.

I was a developer and technical writer in an Agile team with two other students that delivered a calendar and course manager prototype for WordPress. As technical writer, I wrote clear and effective technical documentation such as the project charter, team biographies, user manual, and final report. As developer, I solely built the application’s API and heavily developed the WordPress front end.

I have a great interest in web apps and OOP. I aim to create fast and reliable web services; organized and well-commented code; loosely couple and scalable components; and simple and easy to use UI. Need to see some examples? Have a look at my projects or see my GitHub repositories
